Data Usage - Frequently Asked Questions What is the purpose of this page?

The purpose of this page is to inform users of this site of how their information will be used if they should sign in via social media.

How do I Sign In?

You will need an account on either Facebook or Twitter. You will sign in on this website using one of those social media websites.

How will my information be used?

Your display name, as set by the social media platform with which you sign in, will be used to identify you as the author of comments you leave on venues which you attend. Venue comments are deleted automatically 24 hours after they are posted, and venue information is deleted automatically 1 week after it enters the database.

Your provider ID, as set by your social media platform, will be used to log you in to this website. It will remain hidden.

If you would like to have your identifying information (display name, provider ID) removed from this site, then you may delete your account. Your information will never be used in ways other than what is specified above..
